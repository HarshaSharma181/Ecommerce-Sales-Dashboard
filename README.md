📊 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard
🚀 Project Overview

The E-Commerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to analyze and monitor sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and payment preferences across different regions and product categories.

This dashboard helps businesses gain actionable insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and data-driven reporting, enabling stakeholders to make informed strategic decisions.

🎯 Business Objective

To provide a centralized analytics platform that enables users to:

Monitor overall sales performance
Track profit trends and growth
Analyze customer purchasing behavior
Evaluate product category performance
Understand payment method preferences
Identify top-performing states and customers
